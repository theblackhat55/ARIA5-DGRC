Risk Management Policy - Version 2.0

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This document outlines the comprehensive risk management framework for our organization. It covers identification, assessment, mitigation, and monitoring of operational, financial, and cyber security risks.

CYBER SECURITY RISKS
1. Data Breach Prevention
- Implement multi-factor authentication for all user accounts
- Regular security awareness training for employees
- Quarterly penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
- Incident response plan with 24-hour activation protocol

2. Ransomware Protection
- Daily automated backups stored in immutable storage
- Network segmentation to limit attack propagation  
- Endpoint detection and response tools on all devices
- Regular backup restoration testing

OPERATIONAL RISKS
1. Service Availability
- 99.9% uptime target for critical business systems
- Redundant infrastructure and failover procedures
- Regular disaster recovery testing
- Business continuity planning

2. Third-Party Risk Management
- Vendor risk assessments before onboarding
- Continuous monitoring of supplier security posture
- Contract clauses requiring security compliance
- Regular vendor security reviews

FINANCIAL RISKS
1. Market Risk
- Diversified investment portfolio
- Regular stress testing scenarios
- Currency hedging strategies
- Monthly financial risk reporting

2. Credit Risk
- Customer credit scoring and limits
- Regular accounts receivable analysis
- Bad debt provisioning policies
- Collection procedures and timelines

COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
- SOX compliance for financial reporting
- GDPR compliance for data protection
- ISO 27001 certification maintenance
- Regular internal and external audits
- Annual compliance training programs

RISK MONITORING
- Monthly risk committee meetings
- Quarterly board risk reports
- Real-time security monitoring dashboards
- Annual risk appetite review
- Continuous improvement processes

This policy is reviewed annually and updated as needed to address emerging threats and regulatory changes.
